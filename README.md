# AskGloucester

AskGloucester is a civic AI assistant for Gloucester, Massachusetts that helps residents find answers buried in municipal records. It ingests and indexes documents from the city council, school committee, planning board, and other municipal bodies — meeting agendas, minutes, ordinances, and reports — then lets anyone ask plain-language questions and get grounded, citation-backed answers. The goal is to make local government more transparent and accessible by turning scattered public documents into a single, searchable source residents can actually use.
